About KisanAI

KisanAI is a conversational, AI-based agricultural companion designed for smallholder farmers across India. Powered by advanced Large Language Models and Machine Learning, KisanAI provides contextualized, accurate responses — empowering farmers to make smarter decisions faster.

Whether you need advice on which crop to plant, how to treat a diseased plant, or which fertilizer to use — KisanAI is available 24/7 in English and Hindi.
